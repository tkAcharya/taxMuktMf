# 📈 taxMuktMf: Indian Mutual Fund Tax Harvesting Tool

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
![Local First](https://img.shields.io/badge/Privacy-Local--First-green)

**taxMuktMf** is an open-source browser extension that simplifies Indian income tax planning for equity mutual funds. It helps you stay under the **₹1.25L LTCG tax-free limit** by analyzing your portfolio aging and suggesting precise "Sell & Buy-back" actions.

### 🎯 Key Features
* **CAS PDF Parser:** Automatically extracts data from Section 3 (Valuation) and Section 5 (Aging) of your Portfolio Statement.
* **LTCG Engine:** Precise calculation of harvestable units based on the FIFO (First-In-First-Out) method.
* **Tax-Free Limit Tracker:** Real-time progress bar against the annual ₹1.25 Lakh tax-free bracket.
* **Secure Ledger:** Maintain a local audit trail of harvested gains to re-check your original investment amount.
* **Local-First Privacy:** Your financial data never leaves your browser. No server-side storage.
* **JSON Backup:** Export/Import your harvesting history for multi-device sync.
